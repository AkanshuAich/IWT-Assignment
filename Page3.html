Course Objectives

  • Explain the basics of cartography used in Blockchain
  • Explain design principles of Blockchain
  • Explain consensus algorithm used in distributed systems
  • Explain the basic building blocks of Blockchain
  • Explains the blockchain system by sending and reading transactions
  • Design, build, and deploy a distributed application
  • Different real-life applications of Blockchain

Course Outcome

  • Understand the basic technology used in Blockchain
  • Understand the working principle of blockchain systems (mainly Bitcoin and Ethereum)
  • Able to understand and design any application specific consensus algorithm
  • Design, build and deploy Smart Contracts and distributed applications
  • Integrating the blockchain technology into their own applications/ projects

Modules

Module I (8 Hours)

Introduction: Information, Information security, need of Information Security, Introduction to Cryptology, Hashing

Module II (10 Hours)

Blockchain overview, Working of Blockchain, Blockchain data structures

Module III (12 Hours)

Overview of Smart Contract & Fundamentals of Ethereum, Consensus Algorithms, Applications of Blockchain
