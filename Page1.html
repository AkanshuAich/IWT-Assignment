Number credits: 3 (3-0-0)

Instructor Information

Instructor Name: Sahoo Arun K

Course Objectives

  • Introduce to students the concept of power quality problems.
  • Focus on different kinds of power quality issues and its impact on power system.
  • Introduce harmonic distortion & its source.
  • Study of different techniques for harmonic analysis.
  • Monitoring power quality and power quality improvement.

Course Outcome

  • Learn to distinguish between the various categories of power quality problems.
  • Understand the root of the power quality problems in industry and their impact on performance and economics.
  • Study the effect of long duration voltage variation.
  • Learn power quality monitoring techniques.

Modules

Module 1 (10 Hours)

Terms & Definitions, Voltage Sags & Interruptions, Transient Over Voltages, Long Duration Voltage Variations

Module 2 (15 Hours)

Fundamentals of Harmonics, Filters

Module 3 (5 Hours)

Power Quality Monitoring
