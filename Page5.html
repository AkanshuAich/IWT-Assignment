Course Objectives

  • Introduce the fundamentals, working principles and design concepts of Mobile Communication Systems.
  • Introduce the current trends in Mobile Communication systems

Course Outcome

  • Understand the evolution and fundamental concepts of Mobile Communication systems
  • Understand block diagram and working operation of Mobile Communication systems.
  • Understand the mathematics, atmospheric effects, fading effects etc. on the Mobile Communication systems

Modules

Module I (10 Hours)

An Overview of Wireless Systems, Fundamentals of Cellular Communications

Module II (10 Hours)

Radio Propagation and Propagation Path-Loss Models, Multiple Access Techniques, Modulation schemes

Module III (10 Hours)

Spread Spectrum(SS) and CDMA Systems, Equalization, Diversity techniques, Multiple Input Multiple output (MIMO) systems
